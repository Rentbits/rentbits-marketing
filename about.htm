We deliver the best tools for rental industry pros and killer customer support. For us‚ it’s that simple.

Today, rentbits offers marketing‚ tracking‚ reputation management and resident retention tools for thousands of advertisers and millions of renters across the United States.

In 2006, the original idea was to build a scalable marketing and syndication solution for the rental market. Over the past seven years, the brand and the vision has evolved.

Meet the Board of Directors

Tim Moynihan
Board Member

Craig Smith
Board Member

James S. Gladney
Board Member

Jim Gladney founded Liberty Capital Partners in 1994. He is an experienced private equity investor, venture capitalist and CEO who has spent the past 25 years starting up, buying, and building a broad array of over 50 companies domestically and internationally. His industry experience includes hospitality, business services, distribution, real estate & construction, software, franchising, manufacturing and media. He has served for 15 years as the CEO of operating companies and 10 years as the Managing Partner of private equity firms. Mr. Gladney has served on the Boards of Directors of over 25 companies and chaired numerous boards, audit committees, compensation committees and executive committees. He is a recognized expert in the areas of Mergers & Acquisitions, Corporate Finance, Corporate Advisory, Strategic Planning, Industry Consolidations, Franchising, Real Estate Development, Negotiation, Public Relations and Sales & Marketing.

Mr. Gladney served as the Managing Partner of Park Avenue Equity Partners, a NYC based private equity firm specializing in acquiring middle market companies.

Mr. Gladney was Chairman and CEO of Boston based All Seasons Services, Inc., a leading provider of vending, dining and office refreshment services with over 2000 employees. Mr. Gladney led a management group that acquired All Seasons. Mr. Gladney and his management team then acquired 36 additional companies in the industry executing a successful consolidation strategy. They grew the business from $80 million in revenue to over $200 million and sold the company to a private equity firm, thereby generating a sevenfold return for investors. Nicole Milici
Board Member

Nicole Milici is the CEO of Spada Media, a Providence based marketing communications firm and also serves as Chief Marketing Officer for Liberty Capital Partners, a private equity firm that invests nationally in middle-market companies and technology start-ups.

Mrs. Milici serves on the board of rentbits.com, a Denver based technology company that generates rental property leads for publicly traded REITs and other institutional property owners andmanagers.

She is a member of the Women Presidents Organization and serves on the Board of the American Red Cross.

Nicole is an an award-winning designer, recognized nationally for her creative art direction and targeted marketing strategies. Her designs have won Emmy Awards, Adrian Awards and Summit Awards for the Rhode Island Meeting & Destination Guide, the Rhode Island Travel Guide, and television production of the Bristol, RI Fourth of July Parade. Most recently she was recognized for an Obie Award for the top billboard design in the US.

Milici has worked with several non-profit and charitable organizations including Child & Family, Afri Afya, Water for Cambodia, and Visting Nurses. Milici's portfolio includes pioneering campaigns for government agencies, medical agencies, non-profits, national franchisors, financial institutions and multinational corporations. She has successfully created anddelivered media campaigns on behalf of a myriad of clients including Krispy Kreme, IMAX Theatres, MassMutual Financial Services, RI Builders Association, Foreside, Hyatt Hotels, DoubleTree Hotels and AAA.

Nicole is a graduate of the University of Connecticut and studied for two years in Munich, Germany. She was born in Wiesbaden, Germany.

She has lived in Rhode Island for over 20 years with her husband and four children.
