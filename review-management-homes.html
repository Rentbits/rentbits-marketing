  • Review Management

Track online reviews and social media efforts to stay on top of your reputation and reach more owners.

Eighty-five percent of consumers say they read online reviews for businesses, and half of owners say they use online feedback when choosing their property management.

If you don’t have a review management-and-response strategy in place, if you’re not working to build a positive online reputation, then you’re putting undue strain on your property management team and marketing efforts.

It’s one thing to earn your reputation as a fair and helpful property manager, but your due diligence doesn’t guarantee that prospective owners will know and trust this reputation. That’s why many of the most effective property managers also look to take advantage of current tenants who are willing to post online about their positive experience.

With the easy-to-use dashboard in our review management tool, you can easily monitor and respond to your online reviews--negative, positive, and mixed. And with our Tenant Buddy product, you’ll have an easy and unobtrusive way to persuade tenants to post a review.
